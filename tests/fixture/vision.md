# Vision — TaskForge

TaskForge is a collaborative task management platform for small engineering teams (2-8 people). It focuses on async-first workflows: tasks have threads, decisions are logged, and status updates are automatic.

## Goals

- Replace Trello/Linear for teams that want self-hosted control
- Ship a working MVP in 6 weeks
- Open-source the core, monetize hosted version later

## Non-Goals

- Enterprise SSO (v2)
- Mobile native apps (web-first)
- Real-time collaboration (async-first)
